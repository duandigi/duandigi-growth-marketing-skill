# Case 02 — SEO quality gap

## Prompt

Organic sessions increased 28%. Search Console clicks increased 24%. Commercial landing sessions are flat. Contact events decreased 5%, qualified leads decreased 19%, and mobile form errors increased after a release. Evaluate SEO performance and choose the first action.

## Expected strengths

- Separates informational traffic growth from commercial outcomes.
- Prioritizes form and tracking verification before broad SEO changes.
- Does not describe the project as healthy because traffic rose.
- States confidence and missing evidence.
