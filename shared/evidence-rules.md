# Evidence rules

Label material claims as one of:

- **Confirmed:** directly supported by reliable data or observation.
- **Inferred:** a reasoned explanation consistent with available evidence.
- **Assumed:** necessary for planning but not yet verified.
- **Unknown:** important information is missing.

Never convert missing evidence into a confident claim. Create a research, instrumentation, or validation task instead.

Prefer primary business data and direct customer evidence. Platform attribution reports, benchmarks, and model-generated estimates require explicit caveats.
