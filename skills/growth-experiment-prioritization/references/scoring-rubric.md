# Scoring rubric

Score every dimension from 1 to 10.

## Benefit dimensions

- **Impact:** expected movement in the primary business or customer-value metric.
- **Confidence:** strength of evidence for the problem, mechanism, and implementation.
- **Strategic fit:** alignment with the current growth constraint and commitments.
- **Reusability:** likelihood the learning or asset can be reused across segments or projects.
- **Revenue relevance:** connection to qualified demand, retained value, margin, or durable monetization.

## Cost dimensions

- **Effort:** total design, implementation, coordination, QA, and analysis effort.
- **Risk:** potential harm to users, brand, privacy, accessibility, SEO, platform standing, data, or operations.

## Calibration

- 1–3: low
- 4–6: moderate
- 7–8: high
- 9–10: exceptional

Do not assign 9 or 10 without concrete evidence. Scores are comparable only when reviewers use the same definitions.
