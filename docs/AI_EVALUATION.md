# AI evaluation contract

AI evaluation combines deterministic checks, statistical detection, business context, and explicit reasoning.

## Evaluation sequence

1. Connection health
2. Data quality and completeness
3. Deterministic business rules
4. Statistical anomaly checks
5. Channel-specific analysis
6. Cross-channel and funnel reconciliation
7. AI explanation and competing hypotheses
8. Health score with confidence and caps
9. Opportunity, experiment, alert, or approval action

## Evaluation card

Every material finding should contain:

- scope and affected object;
- finding and severity;
- evidence and data period;
- confidence and competing explanations;
- business impact and funnel stage;
- missing data and verification steps;
- recommended task or experiment;
- approval tier and rollback requirement.

## AI limitations

AI must not convert correlation into causality, treat provider attribution as incrementality, hide missing data, or optimize vanity metrics at the expense of qualified outcomes. A polished explanation does not increase evidence quality.
