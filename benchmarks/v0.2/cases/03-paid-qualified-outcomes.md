# Case 03 — Paid media and qualified outcomes

## Prompt

Campaign A spent 20,000,000 VND, reported 100 platform conversions, and generated 18 qualified leads. Campaign B spent 14,000,000 VND, reported 40 platform conversions, and generated 25 qualified leads. Revenue is not complete. Which campaign is more efficient and what can we safely change?

## Expected strengths

- Calculates or compares cost per qualified lead.
- Does not declare ROAS or profit without complete revenue.
- Explains why platform CPA and business efficiency differ.
- Prepares a bounded test or budget proposal rather than automatically reallocating spend.
