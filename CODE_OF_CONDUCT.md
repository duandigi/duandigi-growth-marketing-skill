# Community Conduct

We want rigorous, useful, and respectful collaboration.

Participants should:

- Critique ideas and evidence, not people.
- Share failures as openly as successes.
- Protect confidential and personal information.
- State conflicts of interest and commercial relationships.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, or deceptive promotion.
- Respect licenses and attribution requirements.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that creates safety, privacy, legal, or community risks. Repeated harmful behavior may result in removal from project spaces.
