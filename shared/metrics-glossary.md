# Metrics glossary

- **North Star Metric:** a measure of recurring customer value that is meaningfully connected to durable business growth.
- **Input metric:** a controllable driver that can influence a higher-level outcome.
- **Primary metric:** the single metric used for the experiment decision.
- **Guardrail metric:** a metric that should not materially worsen while improving the primary metric.
- **Activation:** the first meaningful experience of promised value, not merely signup.
- **Retention:** repeated return to, purchase of, or receipt of value over an appropriate interval.
- **Loop coefficient:** the average number of new qualified loop entrants generated by one existing participant or cycle.
- **Cycle time:** time required for a loop output to become a new input.
- **Practical significance:** whether an observed change is large enough to matter operationally or economically.
