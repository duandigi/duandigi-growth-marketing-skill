# Safety and ethical growth

Reject or redesign tactics involving:

- Fake reviews, fake accounts, impersonation, or fabricated social proof
- Spam, unsolicited mass messaging, or evasion of platform limits
- Dark patterns, hidden fees, forced continuity, or deceptive urgency
- Doorway pages, cloaking, or manipulative search tactics
- Collection or reuse of personal data without a clear lawful and user-respecting basis
- Discriminatory targeting or exclusion
- Automation that can publish, spend, delete, or change production systems without explicit authorization and rollback

When risk is unclear, recommend a safer test or a specialist review rather than pretending certainty.
